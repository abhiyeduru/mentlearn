# üé¨ Admin Quick Start: Managing Landing Page Videos

## Step-by-Step: Add Your First Video

### Step 1: Access Admin Panel
1. Go to your admin dashboard
2. Click **"Landing Videos"** in the left sidebar menu

### Step 2: Add a Video
1. Find the **YouTube video** you want to add
2. Copy the full URL (e.g., `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID`)
3. Paste it in the **"YouTube URL"** field
4. Enter a **Title** (e.g., "Student Success Story - John Doe")
5. Set **Order**: `0` for first video, `1` for second, etc.
6. Check **"Featured"** for the main video (only one should be featured)
7. Ensure **"Active"** is checked
8. Click **"Add Video"**

### Step 3: Add More Videos
Repeat Step 2 for 3-6 videos total:
- 1 Featured video (your best testimonial)
- 2-5 Supporting videos (other testimonials/previews)

### Step 4: Verify on Landing Page
1. Open your landing page in a new tab
2. Scroll to **"WHY STUDENTS & CREATORS JOIN US"** section
3. See your featured video playing automatically
4. Click the circular thumbnails to switch videos
5. Click **"APPLY NOW"** button to test the redirect

## ‚úÖ Video Checklist

Before adding a video, ensure:
- [ ] Video is public on YouTube
- [ ] Video is embeddable (not restricted)
- [ ] Vertical format preferred (9:16) but horizontal works
- [ ] Length: 15-60 seconds ideal
- [ ] Clear audio and good lighting
- [ ] Relevant content (testimonials, course previews, success stories)

## üéØ Recommended Video Types

### Videos 2-4 - Supporting Content

## üìù Admin Panel Fields Explained

| Field | Purpose | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| **YouTube URL** | Link to video | `https://youtu.be/dQw4w9WgXcQ` |

## üîÑ How to Update/Replace Videos

### Edit Existing Video
1. Find the video in the list
2. Click **"Edit"** button
3. Modify any fields
4. Click **"Update Video"**

### Replace a Video
1. Click **"Edit"** on the old video
2. Change the YouTube URL
3. Update the title
4. Click **"Update Video"**

### Remove a Video
1. Find the video in the list
2. Click **"Delete"** button
3. Confirm deletion

## üö® Troubleshooting

### Video Not Showing on Landing Page
1. **Check if Active**: Ensure "Active" checkbox is checked
2. **Verify URL**: Make sure YouTube URL is correct
3. **Public Video**: Video must be public, not private
4. **Clear Cache**: Refresh page with Ctrl+F5 (or Cmd+Shift+R on Mac)

### Video Won't Play
1. **Embeddable**: Check if YouTube video allows embedding
2. **Browser**: Try different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
3. **Ad Blocker**: Temporarily disable ad blockers

### Thumbnails Not Clickable
1. **Multiple Videos**: Need at least 2 videos for thumbnails to show
2. **JavaScript**: Check if JavaScript is enabled in browser
