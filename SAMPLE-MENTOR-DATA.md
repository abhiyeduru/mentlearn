3. **Paste into admin form fields:**
   - Name ‚Üí Name field
   - Title ‚Üí Title field
   - University ‚Üí University field
   - Bio ‚Üí Bio textarea
   - Quote ‚Üí Quote textarea
   - Expertise ‚Üí Expertise field (comma-separated)
   - Achievements ‚Üí Achievements textarea (one per line)
   - Video URL ‚Üí Video URL field
   - Additional Images ‚Üí Additional Images textarea (one per line)
   - Course Sections ‚Üí Course Sections textarea (separate with blank line)
   - LinkedIn ‚Üí LinkedIn field
   - Twitter ‚Üí Twitter field
   - Website ‚Üí Website field

4. **Upload a profile image** (use Unsplash or your own)

5. **Set size preset:** Hero (320√ó320px)

6. **Set position:** 0, 1, 2, etc.

7. **Click "Add Image"**

8. **Repeat** for more mentors
